FERROPOLIS

An industrial metropolis defined by control from above, survival below and a hidden organic foundation beneath both. This page functions as the central world database of the project site.

ArchivesIron GuardSub-Level NetworkTitan-Tree Foundation
World Overview

A city whose official truth is structurally false.

Ferropolis presents itself as a monument to machine permanence. In reality, it depends on buried organic remnants from an older world. The ruling system preserves legitimacy through information control, doctrine and force, but both Elara and Jax expose different parts of that lie.

Archives

The top layer of the city: clean, controlled and devoted to historical sanitisation.

Function

Data regulation

The Archives act as the cerebral cortex of Ferropolis, processing and erasing memories deemed dangerous by the governing doctrine.

Primary Character

Elara

A Data Weaver whose encounter with organic code and lost ecological memory destabilises the city’s official worldview.

Turning Point

Digital Seed

The release of the forbidden image transforms private knowledge into public truth.

Deep Layers

The mechanical underside where noise, risk and hidden structural weakness converge.

Environment

Sub-Level industrial corridors

Dark, oppressive infrastructure built on vibration, heat, pressure and state surveillance.

Primary Character

Jax

A blind sonar-scrapper who interprets the city through illegal audio implants and hears what others cannot.

Operational Tension

Guard sweep / evasion

Discovery is always paired with risk. Proof must be taken under the threat of enforcement response.

Bedrock

The foundation layer where the city’s doctrine collapses under material evidence.

Organic Thump

Jax detects a dense, muted vibration that should not exist beneath a city that claims metal eternity.

Titan-Tree Root

The understructure is not dead geology but the fossilised body of older organic life.

Amber Sap

Golden sap becomes physical proof that buried life persists—and that the city survives by draining it.
